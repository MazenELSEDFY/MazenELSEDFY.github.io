# about

About me

I am a Master of Mathematics (MMath) student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo and a member of the Data Intelligence Lab under the supervision of Professor Renée J. Miller. I also serve as a Teaching Assistant in the David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science.

My research focuses on data discovery, table version identification, data lakes, semantic data integration, and large language models for data management. I am particularly interested in understanding the lineage and evolution of structured data and developing systems that help users discover, relate, and manage large collections of tables.

Prior to joining the University of Waterloo, I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology and Maritime Transport (AASTMT), graduating with distinction. Throughout my academic and professional experiences, I have worked on machine learning, software engineering, database systems, cloud infrastructure, and human-computer interaction projects.

My goal is to contribute to next-generation intelligent data management systems that combine database principles with modern AI technologies.
